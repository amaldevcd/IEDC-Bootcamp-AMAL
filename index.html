HOW TO USE EXPENSO?

This is a simple site build using HTML CSS and javascript. Simply type the expenses and earnings and then click on "Add" button. You can add any number of expenses and earning of your choice in the form provided. You can check your total expenses and total earning by clicking on the "List all" button. You can reset the forms with delete all button. By clicking on the "Balance" button you can see your balance.
